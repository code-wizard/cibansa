about us

Let’s share a small story which might tell more about us, There was once a frog which fell into a container of water, the container was on a electric heater. The frog did not attempt to jump out of the container as it adapted itself to the raising temperature accordingly and stayed in it. As the temperature of the water started rising and reached its boiling point, the frog was no longer able to adapt. The frog tried to jump out of the container but with water temperature reaching its boiling point, the frog was not able to bear it and couldn’t make it. 
 
The lesson to be learnt from 

  • Do the right thing which is relevant to you, staying with the flow sometimes may lead to no man’s land.
  • If we are not sure on what needs to be done, CONSULT.
  • If the skill you possess is becoming obsolete REDEFINE.
  • If the current job doesn’t meet your career goal REFINE and RE-INSTATE. 
  • Create a next version of yourself and be RELEVANT to growing market trends may be your own version of 2.0.
